The only 5-Star Luxury and Business Hotel in the Heart of Secunderabad

Located on the arterial road connecting Hyderabad and Secunderabad at a central downtown location, The Manohar is ideally suited to service both the cities. With close proximity to numerous multinationals and large corporates, the hotel is just 100 metres away from the US Consulate, less than 5 minutes drive from the famed Necklace Road, making it the most convenient location in the twin cities.

From the airport at Shamshabad

  • All buses and cabs from the Shamshabad airport reach Begumpet.
  • It is approximately 30 Kilometers and takes 1 hour to reach the hotel by cab.
  • Take the airport shuttle towards Begumpet, which has a drop point right opposite the hotel.
  • The airport shuttle takes approximately 75 minutes to reach the hotel.

From the Secunderabad Railway Station

  • Several buses and cabs reach Begumpet
  • The railway station is 6 kms from the hotel and takes 20 minutes by cab to reach the hotel.
  • Bus numbers 10L,10P, 10N, 10 P/F, 10R, 10U, 10Y, 10 Y/F, 10Z have a stop right at the hotel.

By Car

  • Begumpet is the heart of the city.
  • It takes less than 30 minutes from any part of the city to reach the hotel.
  • Begumpet road is the main lifeline of the twin cities, so all vehicles ply on this road.
  • The Hotel is the first and only five star hotel enroute to Secunderabad.
