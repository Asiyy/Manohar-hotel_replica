Banquet / Conference Halls

The banquet and meeting halls at The Manohar are ideal conference venues for all those important business meetings, training programmes, marriages, etc. These conference rooms, equipped with all the state-of-the-art audio-visual conference equipments including overhead projectors, projection screens, back projections, laptops, wireless microphones and hi-speed internet access with full IT support are the perfect host to all types of business conferences.

Our Crystal Palace coupled with the sprawling poolside and the lawns are suitable not only for a wedding ceremony but also are perfect for all types of events social or business.
